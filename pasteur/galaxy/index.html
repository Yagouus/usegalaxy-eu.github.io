GCC Registration Open!

[3 May 2019 11:21]

Galaxy Community Conference (GCC) 2019 will be held in Freiburg, Germany 1-6 July and registration is now open. The GCC aims at bringing together Galaxy Users, Developers and Administrators to learn from each other. Tell us about your use case, how we can improve Galaxy, present your work or attend one of the many training sessions. Register now!
