The Freiburg Galaxy Team offers a framework for scientists on e.g. NGS data analyses (RNA-seq, ChIP-seq, Exome-seq, MethylC-seq), genome annotation analyses for eukaryotic and prokaryotic organisms (from gene prediction to functional description), Proteomics and Metabolomics analysis, and the ChemicalToolBoX for analysis of small compounds.

Furthermore, Galaxy has a huge variety of data visualization tools. Galaxy contains more than 800 different single analysis tools and ready-to-use pipelines for different applications.

After registration, all tools and the infrastructure for data analysis are freely available and can be used for creation of your own workflows for your specific projects.

Training

The Freiburg Galaxy Team cares about training.

Twice a year, we organize a week-long workshop on NGS data analysis, here in Freiburg. We also regularly lead training events around the world, covering a wider range of topics such as data analysis, development, and Galaxy administration. You can check our calendar to find out how to join our next workshop.

Unfortunately, we cannot meet the demand for training. For those of you unable to attend one of our training sessions, we offer training materials, freely accessible online at http://training.galaxyproject.org
