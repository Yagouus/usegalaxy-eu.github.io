Mar 14, 2019
Freiburg, Germany
Freiburg devops downtime

Our university data center had a scheduled power test on Thursday (2019-03-14). The day included scheduled network and OpenStack maintenance following the power test, but due to issues discovered in the network, we had to roll back planned network changes, and cancel OpenStack upgrade plans.

This unfortunately resulted in an entire day of downtime of UseGalaxy.eu

There were two ongoing cloud issues which significantly affected our ability to recover from this incident:

  • A storage issue with Ceph (German)
  • And the advertised power test (German)

The Freiburg Galaxy Team is headed by Björn Grüning and is part of the Bioinformatics Group Freiburg, headed by Prof. Dr. Rolf Backofen.
